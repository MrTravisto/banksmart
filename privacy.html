BankSmart does not use cookies unless Google Analytics is added in the future, in which case only anonymous analytics cookies may be used.

Our website includes links to external banks and financial institutions. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites.

4. Data Protection (POPIA & GDPR)

Because BankSmart does not process identifiable personal information, we do not maintain user profiles or customer databases. All user inputs remain on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
